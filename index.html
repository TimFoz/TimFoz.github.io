About Chris

Hello, my name is Chris and I look forward to helping you on your way to your new life in the UK. I am a patient and considerate teacher with a wealth of experience in helping students of all abilities improve their English and adapt to life in the wonderful country which I call home.

My course offers a unique blend of British history, culture and language to help you get the most out of life in the UK. In addition to my groundbreaking online course I can also offer home stays and local tours to fully immerse yourself if British life and culture.

After a successful career in Financial Services, I began following my passion for teaching English in 2017. I have attained a wide range of teaching qualification and have taught at a variety of industry-leading institutions, in person and online.

In my spare time I am a keen local historian and writer. My first book was recently published to great acclaim.
